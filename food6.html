Pav Bhaji is our homage to Mumbai’s bustling street food culture. A buttery blend of mashed vegetables simmered in signature spices, served piping hot alongside soft, toasted pav buns. Topped with a dollop of butter, diced onions, fresh coriander, and a wedge of lemon, this dish offers a flavorful, spicy, and deeply comforting experience — one that transports you straight to the streets of Maharashtra.

Price: 149 Rs.
