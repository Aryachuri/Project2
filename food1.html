Our Pizza Margherita is made using only the finest ingredients to honor its Neapolitan roots. We start with hand-kneaded pizza dough made from premium flour, water, salt, and yeast. The base is topped with rich and tangy San Marzano tomato sauce, followed by slices of fresh mozzarella cheese that melt beautifully in the oven. Fresh basil leaves are added for a burst of aromatic flavor, and the entire pizza is finished with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a pinch of salt before being baked in a traditional wood-fired oven.
