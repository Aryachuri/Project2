Our Roast Chicken is a celebration of rustic elegance, prepared using a whole chicken marinated overnight with freshly crushed garlic, rosemary, thyme, and lemon juice. Slow-roasted to lock in moisture, the skin turns golden and crisp while the inside remains tender and juicy. Served with roasted root vegetables and pan-drippings, this dish offers a hearty and flavorful experience that brings comfort and refinement to your table.
